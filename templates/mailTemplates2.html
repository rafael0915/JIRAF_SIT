REMINDER: EMAIL AWARENESS (CYBERSECURITY RELATED)

Dear Captain,

Good day.

This message is to raise awareness regarding cybersecurity practices.

Please be advised: Do not open attachments or click links from suspicious or unknown senders.

These emails often create a false sense of urgency in the subject line or message to deceive recipients. They are typically phishing attempts designed to steal sensitive information or redirect users to malicious websites that can compromise accounts and data.

*Kindly refer to the file titled "phishing email (sample)" for reference.

If you receive a suspicious email from an unknown sender, please follow these steps:

  1. Send a screenshot of the email for our reference. Do not click/open links or files attached to the email, and do not respond to unknown senders.
  2. Wait for our verification. We will confirm whether the email is legitimate or not.
  3. If confirmed as spam/phishing, block the sender using the attached guides:
    • "block sender (Outlook)" – for Outlook email client
    • "block sender (Thunderbird)" – for Thunderbird email client
    • "add sender to block list (Thunderbird)" – for Thunderbird email client

Note: Spam/phishing email filtering is already enabled on SkyMail and FleetMail clients.

Kindly acknowledge this email for confirmation.

Thank you very much.
